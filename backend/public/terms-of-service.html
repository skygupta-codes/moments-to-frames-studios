2. Booking & Retainer

  • To secure a photography session, a non-refundable retainer fee is required at the time of booking.
  • The remaining balance is due on or before the day of your session.
  • Prices and packages are subject to change. Your rate is locked in once your retainer is paid and contract is signed.

3. Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • The booking retainer is non-refundable.
  • We allow one (1) complimentary reschedule if requested at least 48 hours prior to your session.
  • Further reschedules may be subject to a rescheduling fee of $25 CAD per occurrence.

4. Delivery of Images
